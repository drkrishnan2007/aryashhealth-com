Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how Aryash Health ("we", "us") handles your information on aryashhealth.com, the AI for Clinicians educational platform.

What we collect

We keep data collection minimal:

  • Email address — Only if you subscribe to course updates. Used solely to notify you when new content is available.
  • Anonymous analytics — Page views and general usage patterns via Vercel Analytics. No personal data is collected.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell or share your email with third parties
  • We don't send spam or marketing emails
  • We don't track you across other websites
  • We don't collect health or clinical data on this site

Email communications

If you subscribe, you'll receive:

  • Notifications when new video episodes are published
  • Occasional course updates (no more than once per week)

Every email includes an unsubscribe link. One click and you're removed immediately.

Your rights

You can:

  • Unsubscribe at any time via the link in any email
  • Request deletion of your email by contacting us
  • Ask what data we hold about you

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email feedback@aryashhealth.com
