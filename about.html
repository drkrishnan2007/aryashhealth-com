About This Project

AI for Clinicians is a free educational resource for doctors who want to understand, use, and build with artificial intelligence.

This isn't a course created by AI researchers or tech companies. It's a practising GP's journey — learning alongside you, sharing what works, what doesn't, and what's actually useful in clinical practice.

Why I'm Making This

AI is changing medicine. Most clinicians know they should understand it, but:

  • Expensive courses and fellowships aren't accessible to everyone
  • Academic content often misses practical application
  • Tech-focused resources assume knowledge we don't have

I wanted something different: free, practical, and made by a doctor who actually uses these tools.

What You'll Find Here

Bite-sized video episodes (5-10 minutes) covering:

  • AI fundamentals — what it actually is, no jargon
  • Daily clinical use — documentation, communication, learning
  • Getting better results — prompting techniques that work
  • Beyond chat — automation, voice AI, custom tools
  • Building things — from idea to working prototype

The "Ask"

This content is free, always. If it helps you, I only ask one thing: pass it on. Share with a colleague. Teach someone what you learned. That's how we all get better.
